TURNED ON WHEN YOU GO OUT

Find someone that’s also ready to meet in real life.

Le Libido is the dating app that only works when you’re out in the world. You check into a real location—a bar, a café, even a neighborhood—and the app activates, showing you who else is nearby and open to making a connection right here and right now.

No swiping. No ghosting. Just instant IRL connection with real people, in real places, in real time.

How it works

  • You check into a location to activate the app
  • Choose between Approach Mode or Be Approached Mode
  • Get notified when someone compatible is nearby
  • Tap a photo bubble on the map to make your move
  • Every match is based on real-time proximity + sexual psychology

Why it's different

Most dating apps keep you stuck inside scrolling strangers. Le Libido is built for spontaneity and serendipity.

  • You can’t use it at home
  • You can’t match unless you’re checked in
  • You can’t fake interest or location

Pricing
